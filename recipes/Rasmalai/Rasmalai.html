Description :

Rasmalai is a popular Indian sweet made of cottage cheese balls (also known as chenna or paneer) soaked in a thickened, sweetened and flavored milk.

Ras malai or rossomalai comes from two words in Hindi - ras meaning "juice" and malai meaning "cream." It is a dessert enjoyed mainly in India, with notes of Bengali origin. It's also enjoyed in Pakistan and Bangladesh.

Ingredients :

  • Rasgulla Balls: Store bought
  • Milk
  • Rabdi Mix
  • Almonds
  • Pistachios
  • Saffron
  • Cardamom

Steps :

  1. Squeeze Rasgulla Balls
  2. Prepare Rasmalai Base
  3. Add Rasgulla balls to the Rasmalai base
  4. Finish with adding the nuts
  5. Chill for a couple of hours and enjoy!
